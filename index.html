Game Rules

Rules are simple. Click on the button to start a new game.

One of the two players is automatically selected to start the game.
The player who has a blinking dot to the right of their name will start.

Launch the dice by clicking on it. Once it stops rolling, the number of points displayed will be the score obtained.
This score is added to the number of points on the current round. You can roll the dice as many times as you like.

If a player, after rolling the dice, gets a score of 1, they lose all their current points and their turn ends!

To keep the number of points accumulated and add them to the total points earned in the game,
the player will have to click the button. The next player will then take their turn.

The first player to reach a total of 100 points wins the game!

Good luck to everyone!
