Game's Rule

Rule are simple. Click on the buttonNEW GAMEfor launch a new game

One of the two players is automatically selected to start the game. The player who has a blinking dot to the right of his name will start.

Launch the Dice by clicking on it. Once its spins are complete, the number of points displayed will be the score obtained. This score is added to the number of points on the current round. You can throw the Die as many times as you like.

If a player, after throwing the Die, gets a score of 1, he loses all his current points and passes his turn!

To keep the number of points accumulated and add it to the total points earned in this game, the player will have to click hold when he wishes. The next player will try his luck.

The first player reaching a total of 100 points wins the game!
